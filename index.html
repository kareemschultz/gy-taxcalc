2026 Vehicle Import Tax Information
Tax rates, formulas, Budget 2026 changes, and dealer rules
Gasoline (<4 yrs)
  • 0-1000cc: 35% duty, 0% excise
  • 1001-1500cc: 35% duty, 0% excise
  • 1501-1800cc: 45% duty, 10% excise
  • 1801-2000cc: 45% duty, 10% excise
  • 2001-3000cc: 45% duty, 110% excise
  • 3000cc+: 45% duty, 140% excise
  • VAT: 14% on all
Gasoline (4+ yrs)
  • 0-1500cc: GY$800K flat
  • 1501-1800cc: (CIF+$6K)×30%+$6K
  • 1801-2000cc: (CIF+$6.5K)×30%+$6.5K
  • 2001-3000cc: (CIF+$13.5K)×70%+$13.5K
  • 3000cc+: (CIF+$14.5K)×100%+$14.5K
  • No duty. No VAT.
Diesel (<4 yrs)
  • Under 1500cc: 35% duty, 0% excise
  • 1501-2000cc: 45% duty, 10% excise
  • 2001-2500cc: 45% duty, 110% excise
  • 2500cc+: 45% duty, 110% excise
  • VAT: 14% on all
Diesel (4+ yrs)
  • Under 1500cc: GY$800K flat
  • 1501-2000cc: (CIF+$15.4K)×30%+$15.4K
  • 2001-2500cc: (CIF+$15.4K)×70%+$15.4K
  • 2501-3000cc: (CIF+$15.5K)×70%+$15.5K
  • 3000cc+: (CIF+$17.2K)×100%+$17.2K
  • No duty. No VAT.
Budget 2026
  • Double-cab <2000cc: GY$2M flat
  • Double-cab 2000-2500cc: GY$3M flat
  • <1500cc under 4 yrs: VAT removed
  • Hybrid <2000cc: VAT removed
  • ATVs: All taxes removed
Electric & Special
  • Electric: 0% all taxes
  • G-Plate: US$2,000 flat excise only
  • Motorcycles ≤175cc: 20% duty, 14% VAT
  • Motorcycles >175cc: 20% duty, 10% excise, 14% VAT
Dealer Imports
  • Excise on 1.5× CIF + Duty
  • Applies to vehicles under 4 years
  • Recognised new vehicle traders may have different calculation
  • Per GRA Excise Tax Regulations
Formula (Under 4)
  • 1. Duty = Rate × CIF
  • 2. Excise = Rate × (CIF + Duty)
  • 3. VAT = 14% × (CIF + Duty + Excise)
  • Total = Duty + Excise + VAT
  • Convert to GYD at exchange rate
